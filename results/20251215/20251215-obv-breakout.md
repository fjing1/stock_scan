## 📚 Methodology

### On-Balance Volume (OBV)
OBV is a momentum indicator that uses volume flow to predict changes in stock price.
- When price closes higher, volume is added to OBV
- When price closes lower, volume is subtracted from OBV
- A new OBV high suggests accumulation and potential breakout
